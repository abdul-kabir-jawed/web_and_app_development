Sample Recipe: Classic Margherita Pizza

‚è±Ô∏è Cook Time: 45 minutes üë®‚Äçüç≥ Difficulty: Medium üçΩÔ∏è Serves: 4 people

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup tomato sauce
  • 8 oz fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves
  • Extra olive oil for drizzling

Instructions

  1. Mix warm water and yeast in a small bowl. Let sit for 5 minutes until foamy.
  2. Combine flour and salt in a large bowl.
  3. Add yeast mixture and olive oil to flour. Mix until dough forms.
  4. Knead dough on floured surface for 8-10 minutes until smooth.
  5. Place in oiled bowl, cover, and let rise for 1 hour.
  6. Preheat oven to 500¬∞F (260¬∞C).
  7. Roll out dough on floured surface to desired thickness.
  8. Transfer to pizza stone or baking sheet.
  9. Spread tomato sauce evenly, leaving border for crust.
  10. Add torn mozzarella pieces on top.
  11. Bake for 12-15 minutes until crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  12. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves.
  13. Drizzle with olive oil and serve immediately.
